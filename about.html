Hey there!

 My name is Lucero. I grew up in Arlington Texas and I am fluent in Spanish. Growing up in a traditional Mexican household, it was my first language. When I was in primary school, I attended school in Mexico. Raised by my grandmohter at that time, I learned the value of culture and where we come from. It was the best education I received growing up. In May of 2020, I graduated from Texas Woman's University with a Bachelor's in Business Administration.

  I love Spanish and culture is extremely important. As a native speaker, hearing people speak Spanish is very rewarding. I believe it's important for us to explore different cultures and languages. It allows you to gain new friends and communicate with other parts of the world.

  My goal is to teach you Spanish the easiest way possible and guide you. Whether it is basic expressions or your goal is to communicate during a conversation, I can show you the way. We will start from the very beginning... ABC's and go from there.
